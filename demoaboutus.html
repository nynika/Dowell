Profile

About Us

DOWELL Technologies was established in 1984 at Chennai, India as Dowell Consultants by a group of Dynamic Software Professionals with rich experience in IT, driven by high values and integrity among themselves.

We have positioned our self as an innovative leader in Software Services by consistently adapting to changes in business with all latest technologies. This strategy has enabled DOWELL to successfully execute any IT projects for the clients and bring in lot of values to their business in the past 4 decades.

We realize the importance of creating awareness among clients, about the improved power of Software in their new Business Process & Applications recommend an implementation plan to achieve a sustainable industry standard in all aspects to meet the growing challenges in the fast growing e-world. DOWELL has a long term objective of playing a Responsible Role in the IT Industry by offering reliable, consistent and quality services to clients at a reasonable cost.


Mission

To Make IT simple for the users by delivering Quality, Consistent and Reliable Services to clients at Global standards. To Create Industry for the Next Generation by creating awareness among clients about the untapped power of software in their current and future business.


Our Strength & Technical Expertise:

DOWELL's major strength is in the Design, Development & Deployment of Business Process & Applications using Web Technologies (i.e. JAVA/J2EE, .Net, PHP), Photoshop, Flash, CSS, Dreamweaver, Wordpress, ORACLE DB, MS-SQL, MY-SQL, Oracle Tools, Visual Basic, PowerBuilder, Visual FoxPro, Python, Postgres SQL, Django, React JS, Power BI, Cloud Computing iOS & Android, Ramco ERP Tools and other latest Technologies. Also we have succesfully delivered many legacy Applications to clients by using the technology of last decades like COBOL, DEC products (VAX Basic, VAX COBOL, VAX Fortran, VAX Rdb, DEC FORMS, DTR, etc.), IBM Main Frame (COBOL, CICS, DB2, JCL, Mantis, etc.) & MANMAN a MRP product.
